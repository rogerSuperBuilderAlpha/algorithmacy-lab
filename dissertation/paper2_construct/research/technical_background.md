## Cluster 1 — methodological warrant: importing formalisms across fields (§2 "the borrowing")

The precedent that makes the IIT import disciplined rather than ornamental: management has imported
formal models from the natural sciences before, and the literature is explicit about how to do it
honestly (keep the math, re-interpret the semantics).

- **[anchor] Levinthal, D. A. (1997).** Adaptation on rugged landscapes. *Management Science*, 43(7),
  934–950. The founding import of Kauffman's NK fitness-landscape model from evolutionary biology
  into strategy. *The canonical precedent to cite.* (confirm DOI in citation pass.)
- **[anchor] Robertson, D. A., & Caldart, A. A. (2008).** Natural science models in management:
  opportunities and challenges. *Emergence: Complexity and Organization*, 10(2). **The on-point
  paper**: explicitly analyzes how the NK model (biology) and the Forest Fire model (physics) were
  "imported" into management, and what extensions the ontological differences between natural and
  social systems require. Use to frame the import as a recognized, disciplined move.
- **[key] Marks, P., & Gerrits, L. (2019).** How to use fitness landscape models for the analysis of
  collective decision-making: a case of theory-transfer and its limitations. *Biology & Philosophy*,
  34(2), 21. **The precise defense for our borrowing**: in transferring a formal model from biology
  to social science, "the **syntactic structure of the model can remain unaltered, whilst the
  semantic dimension requires considerable modification**." Exactly our move — import the
  irreducibility mathematics; re-interpret the semantics for coordination; drop the consciousness
  claims. DOI 10.1007/s10539-019-9687-x.
- **[review] Baumann, O., Schmidt, J., & Stieglitz, N. (2019).** Effective search in rugged
  performance landscapes: a review and outlook. *Journal of Management*, 45(1), 285–318. Systematic
  review of 71 NK simulation studies in management since Levinthal 1997 — evidence that a borrowed
  formalism can found a durable research program. DOI 10.1177/0149206318808594. (verify year 2018
  online / 2019 issue.)
- **[support] Ganco, M., & Hoetker, G. (2009).** NK modeling methodology in the strategy literature.
  *Research Methodology in Strategy and Management*, 5, 237–268. On the mechanics and cross-field
  cross-pollination of an imported model.
- **[support] Frenken, K. (2006).** A fitness landscape approach to technological complexity,
  modularity, and vertical disintegration. *Structural Change and Economic Dynamics*, 17(3),
  288–305. Generalized NK with **standards that mediate interdependencies** — a precedent for
  modeling a *mediator* inside a borrowed formalism; resonates with our S node.
- *Foundational source for NK itself:* Kauffman, S. A. (1993). *The Origins of Order.* Oxford
  University Press. (cite as the origin of the imported formalism.)

**How §2 uses this:** the borrowing is standard practice (Levinthal/Kauffman → strategy), and the
honest version keeps the syntax and re-works the semantics (Marks & Gerrits). We import IIT's
irreducibility measure (Φ), re-interpret its semantics for coordination forms, and leave the
consciousness theory at the door — the same disciplined transfer the NK literature models.

## Cluster 2 — Φ / integration as an irreducibility measure beyond consciousness (§2)

The defense that the imported measure is a legitimate, measure-independent account of irreducibility,
used across the sciences, not a consciousness-specific artifact.

- **[direct hit] Balduzzi, D., & Tononi, G. (2008).** Integrated information in discrete dynamical
  systems: motivation and theoretical framework. *PLoS Computational Biology*, 4(6), e1000091. **The
  formal result our computation instantiates**: Φ is low for systems that merely copy/replay, and
  "**strictly modular… systems cannot generate high phi because [they] lack integration**." This is
  exactly why our factoring/dyadic cases score Φ = 0 and the integrated triad scores Φ > 0. Cite as
  the theoretical backing for the worked examples. DOI 10.1371/journal.pcbi.1000091.
- **[foundational] Hoel, E. P., Albantakis, L., & Tononi, G. (2013).** Quantifying causal emergence
  shows that macro can beat micro. *PNAS*, 110(49), 19790–19795. 334 cites. Effective information as
  a measure of how much a system's mechanisms constrain its states — irreducibility/emergence used
  as a general causal measure. DOI 10.1073/pnas.1314922110.
- **[review] Mediano, P. A. M., Rosas, F. E., Carhart-Harris, R. L., Seth, A. K., & Barrett, A. B.
  (2021).** Greater than the parts: a review of the information decomposition approach to causal
  emergence. *Phil. Trans. R. Soc. A*, 379(2212), 20200267. Emergence = information about a system's
  evolution "that cannot be obtained from the parts separately" — the irreducibility framing,
  quantifiable and testable, independent of consciousness. DOI 10.1098/rsta.2020.0267.
- **[key] Rosas, F. E., Mediano, P. A. M., Jensen, H. J., Seth, A. K., Barrett, A. B.,
  Carhart-Harris, R. L., & Bor, D. (2020).** Reconciling emergences: an information-theoretic
  approach to identify causal emergence in multivariate data. *PLoS Comp. Biol.*, 16(12), e1008289.
  Formal downward causation + **"causal decoupling"** — directly resonant with the eliminate-the-dyad
  result (suppressing direct interaction concentrates the irreducible structure). DOI
  10.1371/journal.pcbi.1008289.
- **[robustness defense] Comolatti, R., & Hoel, E. (2022).** Causal emergence is widespread across
  measures of causation. arXiv:2202.01854 (and *Entropy* 2025, "Consilience in causation"). Over a
  dozen independently developed measures of causation all exhibit causal emergence, because they
  share a few "causal primitives" — so irreducibility/emergence is **not a quirk of IIT's Φ**. The
  strongest single answer to "you only get this because you chose Φ."
- **[IIT 4.0 formal] Marshall, W., Albantakis, L., & Tononi, G. (2022).** System integrated
  information. *Entropy*. The system-level φs definition (existence, intrinsicality, information,
  integration) used by `new_big_phi.sia` — the exact quantity our instrument computes.
- **[IIT 4.0 formal] Barbosa, L. S., et al. (2021).** Mechanism integrated information. *Entropy*,
  23(3), 362. The mechanism-level φ underlying IIT 4.0.
- **[philosophy] Negro, N. (2022).** Emergentist integrated information theory. *Erkenntnis*. IIT as
  emergentism, not panpsychism: integrated information as "the constraining power of the system as a
  whole upon itself." Useful to frame the import without inheriting the panpsychist baggage.
- *(IIT 4.0 primary statement — Albantakis, Barbosa, Findlay, Grasso, … Tononi (2023), "IIT 4.0,"
  PLoS Comp. Biol. — to confirm via the harness/citation pass; the foundational IIT 3.0 math is
  Oizumi, Albantakis & Tononi 2014, PLoS Comp. Biol.)*

**How §2 uses this:** Φ is one member of a consilient family of irreducibility/causal-emergence
measures used across physics, biology, and neuroscience (Comolatti & Hoel); the specific result that
*modular systems carry no integrated information* (Balduzzi & Tononi) is the theoretical content of
the dyadic-vs-triadic contrast; none of it depends on the contested consciousness claims.

## Cluster 2b — separating the measure from the consciousness theory (§2 concession)

The one-sentence concession the outline asks for, backed: IIT-as-consciousness is genuinely contested
(panpsychism charge), but the irreducibility *mathematics* is what we import, and it stands on its own
(Negro 2022 on emergentism vs panpsychism; the causal-emergence literature using the measure with no
consciousness commitment; Dewhurst 2020/2021 reading causal emergence as epistemic — relevant to §3).

## Cluster 0 — the imported tool itself: the IIT formalism (web harness, 3-0 verified, all OA)

The canonical, open-access primary statements of the irreducibility measure we compute. All confirmed
verbatim (3 independent verifications each) against the published sources.

- **[the measure, current] Albantakis, L., Barbosa, L., Findlay, G., Grasso, M., Haun, A. M.,
  Marshall, W., Mayner, W. G. P., … Tononi, G. (2023).** Integrated information theory (IIT) 4.0:
  formulating the properties of phenomenal existence in physical terms. *PLOS Computational Biology*,
  19(10), e1011465. **OA.** DOI 10.1371/journal.pcbi.1011465. *The current formalism.* "The
  integrated information of a system is given by its irreducibility over its minimum partition
  (MIP)"; the **integration postulate**: cause-effect power "must specify its cause-effect state as a
  whole set of units, **irreducible to separate subsets of units**" — i.e., it does not factor along
  party lines. This is verbatim the dyadic/triadic distinction the paper needs.
- **[the math, foundational] Oizumi, M., Albantakis, L., & Tononi, G. (2014).** From the
  phenomenology to the mechanisms of consciousness: IIT 3.0. *PLOS Computational Biology*, 10(5),
  e1003588. **OA.** DOI 10.1371/journal.pcbi.1003588. Φ = the distance between the cause-effect
  structure of the whole and that of the partitioned set; "information generated by the whole
  mechanism above and beyond its parts," over the MIP; **φ = 0 means reducible / factors.**
- **[the software we run] Mayner, W. G. P., Marshall, W., Albantakis, L., Findlay, G., Marchman, R.,
  & Tononi, G. (2018).** PyPhi: a toolbox for integrated information theory. *PLOS Computational
  Biology*, 14(7), e1006343. **OA.** DOI 10.1371/journal.pcbi.1006343; arXiv:1712.09644;
  github.com/wmayner/pyphi. "Unfolds the full cause-effect structure of discrete dynamical systems of
  binary elements and computes its Φ"; the MIP is "the partition that yields the minimum
  irreducibility." *This is the exact tool `proxy_audit.exact_phi` wraps.*
- **[clean one-line definition] Zaeemzadeh, A., & Tononi, G. (2024).** Upper bounds for integrated
  information. *PLOS Computational Biology*, 20(8), e1012323. **OA.** DOI
  10.1371/journal.pcbi.1012323. Integrated information = "how much of the causal powers of a subset of
  units in a state **cannot be accounted for by its parts**; if … fully explained by its parts, it is
  reducible and its integrated information is zero." The cleanest sentence to import.
- **[what we DON'T borrow] Tononi, G., & Koch, C. (2015).** Consciousness: here, there and
  everywhere? *Phil. Trans. R. Soc. B*, 370(1668), 20140167. DOI 10.1098/rstb.2014.0167. States the
  consciousness identity (Φmax = quantity of consciousness) — the claim the paper explicitly leaves
  at the door while keeping the irreducibility measure.

## CAVEATS from the adversarial verification (3 claims refuted — do NOT overstate)

1. **PyPhi is NOT "domain-agnostic / any system."** It targets **discrete binary Markovian systems
   specified by a TPM**. Our application-layer matrices *are* exactly that, so we are within scope —
   but frame the tool precisely ("discrete dynamical systems specified by a transition matrix"), never
   as universal.
2. **A single scalar Φ bundles heterogeneous "integration" modes (ΦID).** This does not undermine the
   binary dyadic/triadic verdict (Φ = 0 vs Φ > 0 is robust), but the paper should concede that one
   number simplifies a heterogeneous quantity — state it, do not hide it.
3. **The strong COMPOSITIONAL/ontological reading of irreducibility is contested** (the
   whole-does-not-reduce-even-when-parts-known claim was refuted 0-3 on Hoel; his causal emergence is
   *epistemic*/level-relative). **Implication for §3:** frame the paper's claim as the precise IIT
   formal property — *the cause-effect structure of the observable application-layer TPM is
   irreducible over its MIP* — which is a computable fact about the dynamics, distinct from both
   "the analyst cannot see the mechanism" (epistemic opacity) and from any metaphysical emergence
   claim. Do not overclaim ontological emergence.

## Terminology lock (flagged repeatedly by verifiers)

Small-**φ** (φs) is the irreducibility of a system's cause-effect *state* over the MIP; big-**Φ**
(structure phi) is the sum over distinctions and relations. **Our instrument computes the IIT-4.0
*system* integrated information φs** via `pyphi.new_big_phi.sia(subsystem).phi` (the system
irreducibility analysis), i.e. the irreducibility of the system over its minimum-information
partition. The paper must name this quantity precisely and not conflate it with the structure-Φ.
