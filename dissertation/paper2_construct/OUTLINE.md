# Paper 2 outline: measuring whether a coordination form is triadic

Roger Hunt III
Bentley University

Working outline, v1.

The paper argues that whether a coordination form is triadic or dyadic is an empirical question with a formal answer, and that integrated information theory, a tool built in another field, supplies it. A coordination form is triadic when its application-layer cause-effect structure is irreducible to a partition, and dyadic when that structure factors. The measure decides which competency a form demands: a form that factors is navigated with literacy, a form that does not is navigated with algorithmacy. The paper builds the instrument. It does not yet calibrate it.

---

## 1. Introduction: the form question and why it has a formal answer

Paper 1 argued that triadic coordination requires a new competency; whether a given coordination form is triadic is left as an assertion until something measures it. The introduction states that the distinction between dyadic and triadic is structural irreducibility, that irreducibility is computable, and that integrated information theory is the tool that computes it. It commits to importing a foreign formalism and to defending the import on the merits.

Moves: state the open question Paper 1 leaves. Declare that triadicity is structural irreducibility and that irreducibility is measurable. Name the instrument and the claim it will support.

## 2. The borrowing: integrated information theory as a measure of irreducibility

Integrated information theory computes one thing, the degree to which a system's cause-effect structure is irreducible to its parts, and that one thing is what the dyadic-triadic distinction requires. The section separates the formalism from the consciousness theory it was built to serve, because the import depends only on the irreducibility measure and not on any claim about experience. It establishes the precedent for borrowing formal tools across fields, so the move reads as disciplined rather than ornamental.

Moves: state what Φ measures and what it does not. Concede that integrated information theory is contested as a theory of consciousness, in one sentence, and return: the contested claims are not the imported ones. Cite the organizational precedent for importing formalisms (fitness landscapes from biology, network measures from graph theory) so the borrowing has standing.

## 3. The application layer: where the measure runs

The measure runs over the application layer, the interface through which the two parties coordinate, and not over the algorithm's internal mechanism. The section makes the distinction that resolves the opacity problem: the mechanism is epistemically opaque and unavailable, but the application-layer states the parties move through and the determinations the mediator commits between them are observable. The transition matrix is built over the layer the parties operate in, so the measure never requires the proprietary internals.

Moves: distinguish epistemic opacity (the analyst cannot see the mechanism) from compositional irreducibility (the whole does not reduce to its parts even when the parts are known). Locate Φ on the second and show it computes over the application layer. Establish that the application-layer transition matrix is constructible from observable interaction logs.

## 4. The state alphabet: the entire empirical commitment

The measure stands or falls on how the application-layer states are individuated, because Φ is not invariant to how the states are carved. The section fixes the individuation rule by definition before any computation, and defends the rule against the two competing criteria it absorbs. A new application-layer state begins when the mediator commits a determination that alters its causal disposition toward the parties, and a determination moves the cause repertoire and the effect repertoire together, which is what makes a transition irreducible rather than cosmetic.

Moves: state the individuation rule as a definition. Concede that action availability and visibility feel like state boundaries, in one sentence each, and return: an action that commits nothing leaves the structure fixed, and disclosing an already-committed value changes who can read it without changing what it constrains. Pre-register the rule so two analysts cannot disagree about whether a form is triadic. Take up the continuous-platform case (Uber commits in a stream) as the rule's hardest test and the open problem the paper leaves to the calibration work.

## 5. Worked application: the dyadic limit and the irreducible triad

The measure earns its keep by separating cases the construct says it should separate. The section computes the contrast: a coordination form whose matrix factors into independent links is dyadic and reduces to the parties already visible in the exchange, and a form whose matrix does not factor is irreducibly triadic. The chat with a language model is the dyadic limit, because the model commits nothing that couples a third party. The résumé through an applicant tracking system to a hiring manager is the irreducible case, because the system commits a determination neither party controls and both must reason through.

Moves: construct the application-layer state alphabet for each case under the fixed rule. Show the factoring case yields low Φ and the non-factoring case yields Φ that the worker-system-counterpart partition cannot reduce. Tie the result back to the competency claim: the reducible form is navigable by local, one-hop inference, and the irreducible form forces forward and backward reasoning through the whole triad.

## 6. What the instrument establishes and what it does not

The instrument decides the form question and leaves the calibration question open by design. The section states the contribution precisely: Φ over the application-layer matrix is a formal test of whether a coordination form is triadic, and therefore of whether it demands algorithmacy rather than literacy. It names the two honest limits, the state-alphabet dependence and the absence of an outcome anchor, and hands the second to Paper 3.

Moves: state the contribution as a decision procedure for triadicity. Concede the tractability constraint (the partition search grows fast in state count) as a discipline on granularity rather than a defect. Hand the calibration anchor forward: a Φ value is not yet a difficulty scale until it is validated against an observed coordination outcome.
