## §1 Introduction — the form question Paper 1 begs

**Function.** Open the classification problem; tie back to Paper 1 (coherence); state that triadicity is
measurable irreducibility, decided by a found tool; preview the contribution as a decision procedure.

**Moves (in order).**
1. Recall Paper 1's contrast — the existing constructs (algorithmic competence, platform literacy, the
   rest) measure a *dyad*, a worker's relationship with an algorithm; algorithmacy names the *triad*, a
   worker coordinating with a counterpart *through* a determination the system commits.
2. The hinge: that contrast only does work if an analyst can say which case is in front of them, and
   inspection cannot settle it. Counting parties does not (a third party can be a passive conduit);
   reading the interface does not (a worker "alone with an app" can be bound to an unseen third). The
   distinction has to be **classified**, and the classification runs against appearances both ways —
   false triads and false dyads alike.
3. Therefore Paper 1 generates a question it cannot answer on its own terms: *given an object of study,
   is the coordination dyadic or triadic?*
4. Declare the answer is computed: map the object and its relationships into a transition matrix and run
   integrated information theory — **Φ = 0 means the structure factors along party lines (a dyad,
   whatever it looked like); Φ > 0 means it does not (a triad, whatever it looked like).**
5. Name the contribution in genre terms: not a new measure of "triadicity," but the **decision procedure**
   the construct requires — a method-introduction contribution (Bergh et al. 2022) importing a tool
   mature elsewhere into organizational measurement.
6. Scope: this paper *builds and validates* the instrument and demonstrates the verdict; it does not yet
   *calibrate* Φ to an outcome — that is Paper 3.

**Citations.** Paper 1 (the construct); Bergh et al. 2022 (method contribution); Engel & Malone 2018
(nearest prior — Φ already applied to human groups; flagged honestly, expanded in §7).

**Crystallized closing claim.** *Whether a coordination form is triadic is not a matter of
interpretation but of computation; this paper builds the instrument that computes it.*

**Failure to avoid.** Re-asserting that the form is triadic (the very thing Paper 1 could not discharge).
§1 must position Φ as the discharge, not repeat the assertion.

## §2 The borrowing — Φ as a measure of irreducibility, and why it fits (center of gravity)

**Function.** Carry the paper's argument: the tool is valid and a natural fit. Two pillars + the
consciousness concession + pre-empt the co-development tension + refuse borrowed credibility.

**Moves (in order).**
1. **Pillar A — borrowing formal tools is disciplined org-science tradition.** The canonical case is the
   NK fitness landscape carried from biology into strategy (Kauffman 1993 → Levinthal 1997 → Rivkin 2000
   → Baumann, Schmidt & Stieglitz 2019). The literature is explicit about *how* to do it honestly: keep
   the **syntax**, rework the **semantics** (Marks & Gerrits 2019; Robertson & Caldart 2008).
2. Frame the move in Bergh et al.'s (2022) vocabulary: a **method-introduction across a method's life
   cycle** — Φ is mature in its origin field, early in its organizational life cycle.
3. **Pillar B — natural fit, not analogy.** State what IIT measures: the degree to which a system's
   cause-effect structure is irreducible to its parts; by the integration postulate, cause-effect power
   "must specify its cause-effect state as a whole set of units, irreducible to separate subsets of
   units" — Φ = 0 exactly when the structure factors along subset (party) lines (Albantakis et al. 2023;
   Oizumi et al. 2014; Zaeemzadeh & Tononi 2024). Strictly modular systems carry no integrated
   information (Balduzzi & Tononi 2008).
4. State what non-dyadic coordination *is*: a form is triadic exactly when it does not reduce to the
   pairwise interactions of the parties — when they coordinate *through* a binding determination rather
   than around it (Paper 1). **These are the same formal object in two vocabularies.**
5. Robustness: irreducibility is not a quirk of one formula — across more than a dozen independently
   developed measures of causation, causal-emergence/irreducibility recurs (Comolatti & Hoel 2022).
6. **The consciousness concession, one sentence, then return:** IIT as a theory of consciousness is
   genuinely contested, but the imported object is the irreducibility *mathematics*, which stands on its
   own (Negro 2022 on emergentism vs panpsychism; Tononi & Koch 2015 is the identity claim left at the
   door).
7. **Pre-empt the Lê & Schmid (2022) tension.** Their ORM principle says *develop theory and method
   together*; we borrow Φ as theory-neutral. Resolution: we co-develop method with the **target**
   (organizational) theory — Paper 1's triadic-coordination construct — and are theory-neutral only with
   respect to the tool's **origin** theory (consciousness). With respect to the theory it is *here* to
   serve, the method is co-developed exactly as the principle requires.
8. **Refuse borrowed credibility (Øby 2024).** The warrant for the organizational use is the tool's
   behavior on organizational controls (§5), not IIT's neuroscience validation. Say so plainly.

**Crystallized closing claim.** *Φ is not an analogy for non-dyadic coordination; it is the same formal
object, carried over by the same disciplined move organizational science has used before — and it
answers to organizational controls, not to its origin field's authority.*

**Failure to avoid.** Ornamental borrowing; importing consciousness baggage; implying neuroscience
validates the organizational use.

## §3 The application layer — where the measure runs

**Function.** Locate the measure on observable dynamics, not the proprietary mechanism; resolve the
opacity problem; fix the precise (non-metaphysical) claim.

**Moves (in order).**
1. The measure runs over the **application layer** — the states the parties move through and the
   determinations the mediator commits between them — all observable in ordinary interaction logs (a
   driver's logins/positions/accept-decline and the dispatches returned; a résumé's submission, the
   forward/reject, the callback).
2. This resolves the opacity problem: the mechanism is epistemically opaque and need not be seen; Φ over
   the application-layer transition matrix is a property of the **observable** dynamics.
3. Distinguish three things carefully (caveat 3): **epistemic opacity** (the analyst cannot see the
   mechanism) ≠ **formal irreducibility** (the structure does not factor) ≠ **metaphysical emergence**.
   Keep the §3 claim honest with the emergence literature (Bedau 1997 weak emergence; Hoel 2017; the
   Eberhardt & Lee 2022 critique cited alongside).
4. The precise claim, and no more: *the cause-effect structure of the observed application-layer
   transition matrix is irreducible over its minimum-information partition* — a computable fact about the
   dynamics the parties operate in.
5. The matrix is constructible from logs without proprietary internals — ground in the platform mechanics
   that *engineer* the strict mediator topology: the platform's disintermediation keeps worker and third
   party from direct contact (Rosenblat & Stark 2016; the "you are you and the app" phenomenology, Stein
   et al. 2023).

**Crystallized closing claim.** *The measure asks not what the algorithm is, but whether the coordination
the parties move through factors — and that is a question their observable interaction already answers.*

**Failure to avoid.** Overclaiming ontological emergence; conflating opacity with irreducibility.

## §4 The state alphabet — the single empirical commitment

**Function.** The one load-bearing commitment; pre-register the individuation rule; show it is what makes
the classification objective rather than chosen.

**Moves (in order).**
1. Φ is not invariant to how application-layer states are individuated, so the carving rule is
   load-bearing: get it wrong and the verdict is an artifact of post-hoc state-carving.
2. State the rule as a definition: **a new application-layer state begins when the mediator commits a
   determination that alters its causal disposition toward the parties.**
3. The IIT reading: a determination is a transition that moves the mediator's **cause repertoire and
   effect repertoire together** — that joint movement is what makes a transition irreducible rather than
   cosmetic, and exactly what Φ registers.
4. Defend against the two rival criteria it absorbs, one sentence each then return: **action
   availability** (an option that commits nothing leaves the causal disposition unchanged) and
   **visibility/disclosure** (revealing an already-committed value changes who can read it, not what it
   constrains).
5. Pre-registration = objectivity: fixing the rule before computing is what lets two analysts individuate
   the same states and reach the same Φ.
6. Granularity is the discipline, not a defect: Φ is super-exponential in state count, and the rule keeps
   the alphabet small in a principled way (only determination-bearing transitions count) — the
   tractability limit and the individuation rule are the same discipline from two sides.
7. The hardest case, pre-registered as open: the continuous stream (Uber commits constantly; no natural
   discrete boundary). The paper does not resolve it; it hands it to Paper 3, where an observed outcome
   supplies the window the continuous case lacks.

**Citations.** `state_alphabet.md`; the IIT-4.0 formalism (Albantakis et al. 2023) for the repertoire
reading.

**Crystallized closing claim.** *Fix how the states are carved before any Φ is computed, and the
dyad/triad verdict becomes a fact about the coordination rather than an artifact of the analyst's hand.*

**Failure to avoid.** Letting the carving look post-hoc — which would make the whole verdict an artifact
and sink the contribution.

## §5 The instrument and its validation — controls before use (the visible "Confirm")

**Function.** The methods-genre's visible validation step; validate-on-controls before any worked case;
compute-don't-assert; the terminology lock.

**Moves (in order).**
1. The instrument: a coordination form is modeled as a 3-node application-layer system — Worker (W),
   System/mediator (S), Counterpart (C) — whose state-by-node transition matrix encodes how each party's
   next state depends on all three. The instrument computes **exact IIT-4.0 system integrated information
   φs over the MIP** via `proxy_audit.exact_phi` (wrapping `pyphi.new_big_phi.sia`). Terminology lock:
   this is φs, not structure-Φ (Marshall et al. 2022; Mayner et al. 2018 for PyPhi).
2. Name the genre adaptation once: "Confirm" here is **formal-validation-on-controls** — the instrument
   must return the answers we already know on cases with known structure — not psychometric reliability
   across a sample. This is a deliberate adaptation of Define-Operationalize-Confirm to a formal
   instrument, not an omission.
3. **Control 1 — factoring.** C causally decoupled (W'=S, S'=W, C'=C): system Φ = **0.000** at all
   states; no partition needed — the structure splits {W,S} ⊗ {C}. (The theoretical backing: modular
   systems carry no integrated information, Balduzzi & Tononi 2008.)
4. **Control 2 — irreducible.** Each party reads the other two (W'=S∨C, S'=W∧C, C'=W⊕S): Φ up to
   **0.830**; MIP = the full tripartition {W,S,C} — no partition reduces it.
5. The gate: only once the controls pass does the instrument classify anything contested. Reproducibility:
   the published `phi_instrument.py` regenerates every number.

**Citations.** Marshall, Albantakis & Tononi 2022 (system integrated information); Mayner et al. 2018
(PyPhi); Balduzzi & Tononi 2008 (modular → 0).

**Crystallized closing claim.** *Before the instrument is allowed to classify anything in dispute, it
returns the answers no one disputes — zero where the structure factors, positive where it cannot be
factored at all.*

**Failure to avoid.** Skipping the controls (the genre's cardinal sin); conflating φs with structure-Φ.

## §6 Worked application — the classifier in action

**Function.** The demonstration the fit holds where the construct says it should; the verdicts (not
illustrations); the false triad and false dyad; the generative eliminate-the-dyad result.

**Moves (in order).**
1. Frame: these are *verdicts*, not illustrations. Each case's answer is fixed by the construct in
   advance; the instrument is run to see whether it agrees.
2. **The dyadic limit — chat with a language model.** Two parties; the model commits nothing that couples
   a third (C decoupled). Φ = **0.000** at every state → classified a dyad. It factors to the parties
   already visible.
3. **The irreducible triad — résumé → ATS → hiring manager.** Strict mediator topology: applicant and
   manager reach each other only through the system's committed determination (no direct W–C edge). The
   sweep over the committed determination: AND/OR/NAND/NOR → Φ = **2.000** (MIP {W, SC}); XOR/XNOR → Φ =
   0.500 (full tripartition); a determination that ignores either party → Φ = 0. → classified a triad,
   and the *false dyad* in microcosm (W and C never touch, yet it does not factor).
4. **The false triad — the party-ignoring mediator.** Three parties present, but the determination reads
   only one (f = W or f = C): Φ = **0.000**. Three on the interface, two in the causal structure — the
   tool overrules the eye.
5. **The false dyad — rideshare driver → app → rider.** Presents as a driver↔app relationship (the driver
   only touches the app, cannot see or choose the rider), yet the rider is constitutive of the dispatch.
   Full triad (rider constitutive, S'=W∧C): Φ = **2.000** (MIP {W, SC}). Dyadic model (rider dropped,
   S'=W): Φ = **0.000**. **The verdict turns on the single edge S←C** — whether the determination reads
   the unseen third party. Ground the referent in the platform literature: the triad is explicit (Healy
   et al. 2024; Dieuaide & Azaïs 2020; Stark & Pais 2020) and does not reduce to the two-sided model (Sun
   et al. 2023, *ISR*; Rochet & Tirole 2003; Hagiu & Wright); the no-W–C-edge is engineered (Rosenblat &
   Stark 2016; Stein et al. 2023).
6. **The central, generative result — eliminate-the-dyad (binary only).** Holding the determination fixed
   (S'=W∧C), the form is a triad with no direct channel (**2.00**) and reduces to a dyad with a full direct
   channel (**0.00**). The intermediate magnitude is encoding-dependent (disjunctive 0.83, conjunctive 6.00),
   so the "falls monotonically" framing is withdrawn — only the binary endpoint result is claimed
   (`eliminate_dyad_sweep.py`). Frame it the way the genre wants (Davis, Eisenhardt & Bingham 2007): the
   instrument is *generative* — this computed regularity **produces** a proposition (the platform's
   structural incentive to design the dyad out) that Paper 3 will calibrate. The political-economy
   reading is computed, not asserted: coordination is most irreducible when the dyad is eliminated, so
   the platform's position is constituted by suppressing direct contact.

**Citations.** `results.md` (all Φ values); `research/platform_triad_grounding.md` (referents).

**Crystallized closing claim.** *The instrument does not illustrate the construct; it adjudicates it —
and the adjudication cuts against appearance in both directions, calling a three-party interface a dyad
and a worker-alone-with-an-app a triad.*

**Failure to avoid.** Presenting the cases as illustrations rather than verdicts; leaving the worked
systems as toys ungrounded in real platform mechanics.

## §7 What the instrument establishes and what it does not

**Function.** State the contribution precisely; position it against the corrected open gap; the three
caveats; coherence hand-off to Paper 3.

**Moves (in order).**
1. The contribution: **Φ over the observable application-layer matrix is a decision procedure for whether
   a coordination form is triadic** — and therefore for whether it demands algorithmacy rather than
   literacy. This is the instrument Paper 1's construct required and could not supply from inside its own
   argument.
2. Position against the **corrected open gap** (honest): the irreducibility toolkit has already reached
   social coordination — Φ applied to human groups (Engel & Malone 2018), ΦID in neuroscience (Mediano et
   al.), PID-synergy on multi-agent collectives — but no work has pointed it at the **mediator-bound
   platform triad**, where a committed determination binds a worker and a third party who never directly
   interact. The residual contribution is *application + the constitutive-mediator framing*, not the
   invention of the measure.
3. The three caveats, stated up front because they make the claim credible: (a) the tool applies to
   discrete systems specified by a transition matrix (what PyPhi computes) — not universal; (b) a single
   scalar Φ aggregates heterogeneous integration modes (ΦID; Mediano et al.) — the binary dyadic/triadic
   verdict is robust, but one number simplifies; (c) the irreducibility is a formal property of the
   observable dynamics — distinct from epistemic opacity and making no metaphysical emergence claim.
4. The state-alphabet dependence (named in §4, not hidden) and the tractability constraint (a discipline
   on granularity, not a defect).
5. The outcome anchor handed forward: a Φ value is not yet a difficulty scale until validated against an
   observed coordination outcome — that, with the continuous-stream granularity, is Paper 3.
6. Coherence (explicit, against Morse's "feigning independence"): this middle paper operationalizes Paper
   1's triadic/dyadic distinction and hands Paper 3 the calibration anchor; the shared object across all
   three is the irreducibility of coordination to its parts.

**Crystallized closing claim.** *Φ over the application layer decides whether a coordination form is
triadic; it does not yet say how hard that form is to navigate — and that, by design, is where Paper 3
begins.*

**Failure to avoid.** Overclaiming (universality, calibration, metaphysics); and feigning independence
from the dissertation arc instead of earning the bridge.
