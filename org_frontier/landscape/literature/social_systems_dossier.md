# Φ on social systems — a sourced dossier (multi-database)

A deeper, multi-database literature sweep (arXiv, PLOS, PNAS, RePEc, philosophy archives, library
catalogs) confirming and refining the [`deep_research_report.md`](deep_research_report.md) white-space
claim. This is the citation backbone for org_frontier's thesis and the probe loop's literature
engagement (`../../probes/PROBES.md`, probes 44–48).

## Headline

**The strict white-space survives:** no published or preprint study applies EXACT Φ (PyPhi on the
full cause-effect structure) to any human social, organizational, economic, team, or coordination
system. The only exact-IIT applications to *collectives* are to animal systems (fish schools).
Estimated-Φ-on-social-systems is essentially two studies (Engel & Malone 2018; Watson et al.
2025/2026). Adjacent measures (O-information, PID, causal emergence) are better populated for
economic/network data; **ΦID has never been applied to any social/behavioral/economic series** — a
clean, citable gap.

## Buckets

### 1 — EXACT Φ / PyPhi on human social systems: NONE FOUND
The nearest exact-PyPhi work is on animal collectives — the key precedent for a dyadic/triadic-vs-
larger transition:
- Niizato et al. (2020a). Finding continuity and discontinuity in fish schools via IIT. *PLOS ONE*
  15(2):e0229573. doi:10.1371/journal.pone.0229573. **[EXACT Φ, IIT 3.0/PyPhi]** A Φ-distribution
  **discontinuity emerges at school size four**, not seen in mutual information or transfer entropy.
- Niizato et al. (2020b). Four-types of IIT-induced group integrity of *P. altivelis*. *Entropy*
  22(7):726. doi:10.3390/e22070726. **[EXACT Φ]** 3→4 discontinuity absent in mutual information.
- Niizato et al. (2024). Information structure of heterogeneous criticality in a fish school.
  *Scientific Reports* 14:29758. doi:10.1038/s41598-024-79232-2. **[EXACT Φ]** Φ maximized at
  criticality.

### 2 — ESTIMATED Φ on human groups/teams/markets (the only two)
- Engel & Malone (2018). Integrated information as a metric for group interaction. *PLOS ONE*
  13(10):e0205335. doi:10.1371/journal.pone.0205335. **[ESTIMATED Φ proxy, Barrett–Seth lineage]**
  4-person work groups, Wikipedia editors, Internet traffic. Higher measured Φ → higher collective
  intelligence / article quality; Internet Φ rose over six years. (arXiv:1702.02462.)
- Watson et al. (2025/2026). Information integration and the latent consciousness of human groups.
  *Chaos, Solitons & Fractals*. ScienceDirect PII S0960077925016406. **[ESTIMATED Φ]** LaLiga
  soccer, player position data; non-zero Φ correlates with top teams and shots on goal. *DOI/year/
  exact-estimator UNVERIFIED — paywalled; verify on the publisher page before citing.*

### 3 — Conceptual: IIT ↔ group agency
Schwitzgebel (2015, *Phil. Studies* 172:1697); Kammerer (2015, *Philosophia* 43:1047); Schwitzgebel
(2016 reply, *Philosophia* 44:877); **List (2018, *Noûs* 52:295 — uses IIT to argue group agents
likely lack phenomenal consciousness)**; Kramer (2021, *Neuroethics* 14:437 — argues List's IIT
rejection is too quick). Sandelands & Stablein (1987, "organization mind") — pre-IIT, **[NOT IIT]**.

### 4 — Adjacent formal measures (irreducibility / emergence)
- Causal emergence / effective information: Hoel, Albantakis & Tononi (2013, *PNAS* 110:19790);
  Hoel (2017, *Entropy* 19:188); Klein & Hoel (2020, *Complexity* 8932526 — Zachary's karate club,
  a social network); Comolatti & Hoel (2025, *Entropy* 27:825).
- O-information / PID / ΦID: Rosas et al. (2019, *Phys. Rev. E* 100:032305 — O-information);
  Williams & Beer (2010, arXiv:1004.2515 — PID); Mediano et al. (2019, arXiv:1909.02297 — ΦID;
  PNAS successor 2025 doi:10.1073/pnas.2423297122); Scagliarini et al. (2020, *Entropy* 22:1000 —
  PID/synergy on global financial markets).

### Foundational IIT / PyPhi
