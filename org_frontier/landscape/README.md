# landscape — how the world uses PyPhi

A survey of PyPhi usage across research communities. The goal is to know the field before building
in it: who computes integrated information with PyPhi, in what disciplines, on what kinds of
systems, with what extensions, and what open application gaps remain. Organizational-theory and
social-system applications are flagged explicitly, because they are this lab's target.

## Question

What is PyPhi used for, by whom, and where are the open application gaps — especially outside
neuroscience, in social, organizational, and artificial systems?

## Result slot

- `literature/deep_research_report.md` — the survey: ~25–30 key sources with full citations, the
  application map (field → systems → what Φ is used to claim), and an explicit gap statement.
- `literature/references.bib` — bibliography, open-access flagged.
- `literature/notes/` — per-paper ingestion notes.
- `SURVEY_FINDINGS.md` — the synthesis: the usage map, the underused directions, and a shortlist of
  candidate organizational-theory applications to pursue next.
- `../essays/literacy_or_algorithmacy.md` — the public-facing writeup (Substack essay): the usage
  map plus the theory that exact Φ decides whether a coordination form demands literacy or
  algorithmacy.
