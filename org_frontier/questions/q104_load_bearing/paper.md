# Q104 — The load-bearing distinction: where the integration sits and what it hangs on

## Question

Q93 found the verdict fragile to a single bit-flip. Its cause-effect analogue asks which element of the
structure carries the integration — which distinction holds the φ, and which edge, removed, collapses it.
Q99 found the integration lives in a binding mechanism with φ_d equal to the system Φ; this confirms that
the binding distinction is the load-bearing one and asks whether the minimal triad has any redundancy.

## Method

The read-recipient triad. Its distinctions are ranked by φ_d, and the verdict is recomputed after knocking
out each of its four reads in turn: the mediator's read of the recipient, the mediator's read of the worker,
the worker's read of the mediator, and the recipient's read of the mediator.

## Results

The binding distinctions carry the integration: {M} → {E, R} and {E, R} → {M} each have φ_d = 2.0, equal to
the system Φ, while the singletons {E} → {M} and {R} → {M} each have 0.5. Every edge knockout collapses the
verdict to dyadic.

| distinction | φ_d |
|---|---|
| {M} → {E, R} | 2.0 |
| {E, R} → {M} | 2.0 |
| {E} → {M} | 0.5 |
| {R} → {M} | 0.5 |

All four edge knockouts give dyadic Φ = 0.

| | result |
|---|---|
| H1 load-bearing distinction is binding, φ_d = Φ | confirmed |
| H2 singletons carry less than Φ | confirmed |
| H3 mediator's read is load-bearing | confirmed |
| H4 every edge is load-bearing | confirmed |

## Interpretation

The integration is concentrated and unredundant. It is concentrated in the binding distinction: the
mediator distinguishing the parties jointly carries the whole system Φ, while each party individually
carries a quarter of it. The cause-effect structure quantifies the law that the whole is irreducible to its
parts — the joint mechanism holds 2.0, the parts 0.5 each, so the binding is four times any single party's
contribution and the parts do not sum to it. This is the structural reading of the law that a triad is one
irreducible joint determination, not a collection of pairwise links.

It is unredundant because every edge is load-bearing. Removing the mediator's read of a party collapses the
verdict, as expected, but so does removing a party's read of the mediator. The minimal triad is a loop in
which each of the four reads is necessary, and there is no edge whose loss the others absorb. This sharpens
Q93's finding that the triad sits one edit from collapse: every edit collapses it, not merely some. The
coordination concentrates its integration in one binding mechanism and hangs that mechanism on every edge,
which is the structural reason the verdict toggles under a single perturbation.

The result closes the structure wave on the question it opened. Q99 found that the integration lives in a
binding mechanism; Q104 confirms that mechanism is load-bearing and shows the parts carry a quarter each,
giving the law a quantitative cause-effect form. The minimal coordination is structurally as tight as it can
be: maximal concentration of integration, zero redundancy of support.

## Limitations

In-silico; Boolean models with the exact cause-effect structure and verdict. One form, the minimal mediated
triad. A larger triad with redundant paths (a ring, a market) would not have every edge load-bearing, so
the no-redundancy result is specific to the minimal form and is not a claim about all triads. The knockouts
are four named edge removals; the full structural margin over all perturbations is Q93's.
