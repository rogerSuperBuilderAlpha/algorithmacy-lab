# Q103 — Stage 1 review: the structure under the operations

## The question

The program established what its operations do to the verdict and membership: substitution collapses the
triad, a tracking memory shifts the core to {M, R, Mem}, delegation puts the triage agent in the core. What
the operations do to the cause-effect structure is unread. Whether the structure collapses under
substitution and restructures under memory and delegation is the integrative question that connects the
structure wave to the operation sweeps.

## What the lab already knows that bears on this

- **Substitution collapses the triad (Q70, Finding 5).** The verdict goes dyadic. The structure should
  empty.
- **A tracking memory shifts the core to {M, R, Mem} (Q92); delegation puts the triage agent in the core
  (Q68).** The structure may restructure to admit the new party.
- **The whole-system verdict and the major complex can diverge (Q74).** The structure may inherit this
  split.

## The gap

No study reads the cause-effect structure under the operations. Whether it collapses under substitution and
restructures under memory and delegation, or whether the whole-system structure diverges from major-complex
membership, is uncomputed.
