# Q99 literature — reading the structure, not the number

## What the program has read so far

The lab has read coordination through the scalar verdict and through major-complex membership. Its
cause-effect-structure work (probes 36, 93, 102) counted distinctions and relations and found the counts
classify the verdict at high accuracy. None of it read the structure as meaning: which mechanism carries
the integration, what it specifies, how the parts and the whole relate.

## The structure IIT 4.0 unfolds

For each subset of elements (a mechanism), IIT 4.0 specifies a cause and an effect — a distinction — with
its own φ_d, and specifies relations among distinctions over shared purviews (Albantakis et al. 2023). The
structure is the system's "form" in IIT's sense, the part of the theory that distinguishes it from a scalar
complexity measure. A coordination's structure should therefore say what the coordination is, not only that
it is irreducible.

## The dual pair

The read-recipient triad's structure contains a spanning distinction — the mediator's mechanism with the
two outer parties as its purview — and a joint distinction — the two outer parties' mechanism with the
mediator as its purview. The two are duals. If this pair is the common shape across joint determination,
depth, and a market, then the cause-effect structure gives the third party a face: it is the joint
mechanism, the whole that specifies a part and is specified by it. Whether the shape is universal across
topologies, or whether different triads carry different structures, is the question.

## Method context

The structure is read with PyPhi's `phi_structure`, the same call the `structure_suite` uses, at the
integrating state, on forms already on the record.
