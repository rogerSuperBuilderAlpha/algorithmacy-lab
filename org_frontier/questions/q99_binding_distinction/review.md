# Q99 — Stage 1 review: reading the cause-effect structure

## The question

The program reads coordination through the scalar verdict and major-complex membership. The cause-effect
structure that IIT 4.0 unfolds (the distinctions and relations, what each mechanism specifies) has been
used only to count features as a predictor, never read as meaning. Whether the structure carries a common
shape across triads, and what that shape says, is the question that opens the cause-effect-structure wave.

## What the lab already knows that bears on this

- **Cause-effect-structure counts classify the verdict (probes 36, 93, 102).** Triadic forms carry more
  distinctions and relations, and the counts separate the verdict. The counts are not the structure.
- **The law: triadic iff every party is bound into one irreducible joint determination (Findings).** If the
  structure has a face, the joint determination should appear in it as a joint mechanism.
- **Depth, breadth, and the market are all triadic (Q64, Q66, Q85).** Whether they carry the same structure
  or different ones is the open question.

## The gap

No study reads the cause-effect structure as a portrait. Whether every triad carries a dual binding pair, or
whether different topologies carry different structures the verdict hides, is uncomputed.
