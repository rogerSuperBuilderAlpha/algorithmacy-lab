# Q73 — Stage 2 deep research: synthesis

This study synthesises Q63-Q72 and draws on the same literatures (shared `references.bib`). The
construct is algorithmacy: the competence of coordinating with another party through a system that
interprets both sides and commits outcomes neither controls. The program reads its structural signature,
irreducibility, with exact integrated information [oizumi2014phenomenology; albantakis2023iit4;
mayner2018pyphi].

The outreach line connects four established literatures to that signature: costly signaling, why an
expensive tailored message can carry information [spence1973job; zahavi1975mate; grafen1990biological];
the economics of spam, why cheap mass sending factors [rao2012economics]; AI-mediated communication, how
agents on both sides change the exchange [hancock2020aimc; jakesch2019aimc]; and coordination theory, the
mediator as part of the structure [malone1994interdisciplinary]. Across Q63-Q72 the structural reading
held together into a single law, stated here. The synthesis is in-silico and inherits the program's
validation gap [axtell1996aligning]; the pillars were each pre-registered and tested in their own study
[chamberlin1965method; platt1964strong; brodeur2024preregistration].
