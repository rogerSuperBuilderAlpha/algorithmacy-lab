# Q102 — Stage 1 review: the relation skeleton

## The question

Q100 found that relation count does not track Φ, and that a mediated breadth market carries 128 relations
against a minimal triad's 8. A count is a summary. The question is the shape behind it: over which purviews
do a coordination's relations concentrate, and why does the count grow.

## What the lab already knows that bears on this

- **The breadth market carries 128 relations at Φ=3 (Q100).** An order of magnitude more than the ring's 14
  at Φ=4. The count to explain.
- **Triads come in mediated and symmetric kinds (Q99).** The mediated kind has a single binder; the
  symmetric kind two groups. The relation shape may differ by kind.
- **Relations bind distinctions over shared purviews (IIT 4.0).** The relation structure is a hypergraph
  over the distinctions, whose shape says how the parts relate.

## The gap

No study reads the relation structure's shape. Whether mediated coordination concentrates relations on a
hub, whether symmetric coordination spreads them, and what explains the breadth market's count, is
uncomputed.
