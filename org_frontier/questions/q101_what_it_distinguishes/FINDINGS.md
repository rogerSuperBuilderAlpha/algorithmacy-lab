# Q101 findings — coordination distinguishes the all-present, determination-firing state

Three hypotheses confirmed, one refuted by the mediated/symmetric split. The binding distinction of a
mediated triad specifies a joint state of the parties, and that state is the all-present configuration where
the determination fires. Dyadic forms distinguish no joint state. The content of coordination is the
distinguishing of the joint-success state.

## What it says

Coordination distinguishes a joint configuration of the parties, not a single party's state. The
read-recipient triad's binding distinction has the two parties as its purview and specifies the state
(1, 1): the mediator tells apart precisely the configuration where both parties are present (H1). The state
it distinguishes is the one where the joint determination fires — the all-present configuration where
coordination succeeds (H2). The all-required market reads the same way, its binding distinction specifying
(1, 1, 1) over the worker and both recipients. This is the content of coordination, read from the structure:
the system is about the joint-success state.

Dyadic forms distinguish no joint state. The broadcast and one_shot have no binding distinction at all (H3):
without a mechanism that specifies a joint configuration of the parties, the form distinguishes only
single-party states, and there is no joint-success configuration for it to be about. The joint-state
distinction is the semantic signature of coordination, present in the triad and absent in the dyad.

The all-present content is uniform, but the single-party binding distinction is not. H4 is refuted because
the required market, a symmetric triad, has no single-party binding distinction — its structure is the
joint-to-joint dual {E, C} → {M1, M2}, with no lone mediator (consistent with Q99). Its joint distinction
still specifies the all-present state (1, 1), so the content is the same; what differs is the shape that
carries it. The mediated triad distinguishes the all-present state through a single binder; the symmetric
triad distinguishes it through two mutually-binding groups. The verdict that coordination is about its
joint-success state holds for both, while the structure that states it differs by kind.

## Caveats

- **Mixed result.** H1, H2, H3 confirmed; H4 refuted by the mediated/symmetric split, which Q99 found.
- **Conjunctive determinations.** The forms read here fire at the all-ones state; a determination that fired
  at a different configuration would specify that configuration instead, which is untested.
- **Three triadic forms.** Read at the integrating state. The content is demonstrated, not swept.
- **In-silico.** Boolean models, exact cause-effect structure.
