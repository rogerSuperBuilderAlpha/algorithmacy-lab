# Q106 literature — a design vocabulary

## From distance to operations

Q105 found the dyad/triad boundary thin and the build usually at a party's read, restoring liveness. A
distance is a measure; a designer needs operations — named moves with known effects. The law gives the
candidate vocabulary: a party is bound into the triad by the mediator reading it (binding), by the party
keeping itself live (liveness), and by being jointly required rather than substitutable (requirement). Each
condition can be supplied or removed, giving a build and a break operation per lever.

## The expected catalog

If the law's conditions are the levers, the operations should partition into three pairs, each build taking
a dyad to a triad and each break the reverse, and the build and break of a lever should be inverses. The
catalog would then be the law read as a set of moves, the bridge from the descriptive verdict to a
design-operations vocabulary the dissertation's third paper needs.

## Method context

The operations are named edits between forms already on the record, read by the standard verdict, with the
build and break of each lever constructed as inverses.
