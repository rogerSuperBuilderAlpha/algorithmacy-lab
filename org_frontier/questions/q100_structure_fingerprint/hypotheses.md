# Q100 — Stage 3 hypotheses (fixed before computation)

Four hypotheses on whether the cause-effect structure fingerprints the coordination kind. Q99 found that
triads come in structural types — mediated, symmetric, distributed. This reads a richer fingerprint
(distinction and relation counts, maximal order, dual type) for a keystone triad of six coordination kinds
and asks whether the structure separates kinds the scalar verdict calls identical, and whether two forms
with equal Φ can carry different structure. Written and committed before the keystone set is read. The
read_recipient fingerprint (mediated, Φ = 2, 4 distinctions, 8 relations) is the instrument control.

## H1 — The keystones have distinct fingerprints

- **Claim:** Among the six keystone triads, at least four carry pairwise-distinct fingerprints on
  (n_distinctions, n_relations, max_order, dual_type).
- **H0:** Fewer than four are distinct; the structure collapses the kinds together.
- **Predicted outcome:** at least four distinct. H0 refuted. The structure is a finer classifier than the
  binary verdict.

## H2 — The dual-type taxonomy partitions the keystones

- **Claim:** At least two of the three dual types (mediated, symmetric, distributed) appear among the
  keystones.
- **H0:** All keystones share one dual type.
- **Predicted outcome:** at least two types. H0 refuted (extending Q99).

## H3 — Equal Φ, different structure

- **Claim:** Two keystones have equal system Φ but different fingerprints.
- **H0:** Equal Φ implies equal fingerprint.
- **Predicted outcome:** a Φ-degenerate pair with different structure exists — the structure carries more
  than the scalar verdict. This is the study's central claim.

## H4 — Structural richness scales with the coordination

- **Claim:** Across the keystones, the relation count is positively rank-correlated with Φ (Spearman
  ρ > 0.5).
- **H0:** Relation count does not rise with Φ.
- **Predicted outcome:** positive correlation — larger or stronger coordinations carry richer structure.
  Genuinely uncertain: structure could be set by type rather than magnitude.
