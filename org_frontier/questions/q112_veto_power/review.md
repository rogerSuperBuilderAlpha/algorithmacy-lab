# Q112 — Stage 1 review: veto power

## The question

Q104 found every edge of the minimal triad load-bearing; Q111 found value concentrated at the mediator.
Which parties hold a veto, able to collapse the coordination by withdrawing, and does the veto track the
value? If everyone can break it but only the mediator captures it, the power to destroy and the power to
capture are distinct.

## What the lab already knows that bears on this

- **Every edge of the minimal triad is load-bearing (Q104).** Each party's loss collapses it, so every core
  party should hold a veto.
- **Value concentrates at the mediator (Q111).** Two-thirds against a sixth. If the veto is universal,
  value and veto come apart.
- **A spectator factors the whole system but leaves the major complex (Q74, Q75).** Its veto over the core
  should be none.

## The gap

No study reads veto power or compares it to value capture. Whether every core party can collapse the
coordination, and whether that universal veto coexists with concentrated value, is uncomputed.
