# Q116 — Stage 2 literature: strategic value against structural contribution

## The question in two literatures' terms

Q116 asks whether two independently-motivated quantities are the same: the Shapley value of a party in the
coordination game (cooperative game theory) and the φ_d of a party's distinction in the cause-effect
structure (integrated information theory). The first is a strategic measure, the average marginal worth of a
party across coalitions; the second is a structural measure, the irreducibility of what the party's mechanism
specifies. That the lab's value wave and its cause-effect wave might be measuring one thing from two sides is
the closing conjecture.

## What each side establishes

- **The Shapley value is the unique fair allocation.** It is the only value satisfying efficiency, symmetry,
  null-player, and additivity (Shapley 1953). It redistributes a coalition's worth to its members by their
  average marginal contributions, so a party's value reflects how the whole depends on it across every
  ordering.
- **φ_d measures a distinction's irreducibility.** In IIT 4.0 each mechanism that specifies a cause and an
  effect contributes a distinction with integrated information φ_d; the structure's distinctions and their
  relations compose the Φ-structure (Albantakis et al. 2023). φ_d is a local, structural quantity, fixed by
  the mechanism and its purview, not by any game over coalitions.
- **The two need not coincide.** A marginal-contribution value averages over removals of a party; a
  distinction's φ_d is read off the intact structure. There is no general theorem equating a cooperative
  value with a local structural weight, so any agreement is a finding about this class of systems, not a
  mathematical identity.

## The gap Q116 fills

The lab computed Shapley values (Q111-Q115) and φ_d distinctions (Q99-Q104) separately. Whether the two
measures agree, and where they part, was never tested. Q116 puts them side by side across a panel, asking
whether structural depth and strategic value are one quantity, the same in rank, or the same only in the
minimal case.
