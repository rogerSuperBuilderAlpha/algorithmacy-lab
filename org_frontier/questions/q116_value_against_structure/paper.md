# Q116 — Strategic value is structural depth, smoothed

## Question

The political-economy wave measured each party's strategic value, its Shapley value of integration (Q111);
the cause-effect wave measured each party's structural contribution, the φ_d its own distinction carries
(Q99, Q104). This closing study asks whether the two are the same quantity, and where they part.

## Method

Two normalized readings of each party's share of the coordination: structural (the φ_d of the party's own
singleton distinction in the cause-effect structure) and strategic (the party's Shapley value of subsystem-Φ).
Both are computed across a panel: the read-recipient triad, the bidirectional principal of Q114, and the
all-required market of Q85 at N = 2 and N = 3.

## Results

The two coincide on the triad and the N = 2 market, rank the parties identically on all four forms, and
diverge in magnitude on the principal and the N = 3 market, where the structural measure concentrates value
on the dominant party.

| form | party | structural φ_d share | strategic Shapley share |
|---|---|---|---|
| triad | M | 0.667 | 0.667 |
| triad | E / R | 0.167 | 0.167 |
| principal | M | 0.800 | 0.583 |
| principal | E / R / P | 0.067 | 0.139 |
| market N=2 | each | 0.250 | 0.250 |
| market N=3 | E / C | 0.286 | 0.258 |
| market N=3 | each agent | 0.143 | 0.161 |

| | result |
|---|---|
| H1 the two coincide on the minimal triad | confirmed |
| H2 the two rank the parties identically on every form | confirmed |
| H3 the two diverge cardinally on the principal | confirmed |
| H4 structure over-concentrates where they diverge | confirmed |

## Interpretation

In the minimal coordination, structural depth and strategic value are one number. On the read-recipient triad
each party's normalized singleton φ_d equals its Shapley share to three decimals, the mediator at 0.667 on
both readings and each outer party at 0.167. The φ_d is read off the intact cause-effect structure with no
game over coalitions in view, yet it already encodes what each party is worth in the coordination game. The
two waves of the program, the cause-effect structure and the political economy of value, measure one thing
from two sides in the small.

The order of value is structural in every coordination. Across all four forms the two measures rank the
parties identically: the deepest distinction belongs to the most strategically valuable party, and no pair is
ever ordered oppositely. The mediator outranks the outer parties on both readings; the unique market parties
outrank the agents on both. A party's structural depth fixes where it stands in the distribution of value
even on the forms where it does not fix the size of its share, so structure predicts the order of value
without exception in the panel.

The magnitudes part once parties are added, and they part with a direction. On the principal the mediator
carries 0.800 of the structure but captures 0.583 of the value, a gap of 0.217; the same gap, smaller,
appears in the N = 3 market. Where the two diverge, the dominant party's structural share exceeds its
strategic share and the periphery's strategic share exceeds its structural one. The φ_d localizes depth in
each party's own distinction, so a party the mediator reads enlarges the mediator's purview and inflates its
distinction. The Shapley value instead credits each added party for the marginal contributions it makes
across coalitions. Strategy redistributes outward what structure piles on the center.

The strategic value is the structural depth, smoothed by marginal contribution. The Shapley value is what the
φ_d distribution becomes once each party is also credited for the coordination it lets the others sustain,
which is why the two share a ranking and coincide whenever no party's depth is inflated by another's presence.
The closing result joins the wave's two halves: structural depth and strategic value are the same order
everywhere and the same number in the small, and the gap between them in the large is itself structural.

## Limitations

In-silico; exact subsystem Φ and φ_d. The structural reading is the singleton φ_d specifically; other
structural weights order the parties differently and were not the pre-registered measure. The panel spans
four forms, and the rank agreement, though exact on all four, is not proven for every coordination. The
Shapley coalition value is the subsystem Φ at the all-ones state.
