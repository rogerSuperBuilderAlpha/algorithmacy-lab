# Research narrative: how the program has progressed, and a path forward

A synthesis of the lab's experimental record through Q73 (237 probes, 27 questions, the eight structural
findings), and a proposal for what to explore next. In-silico throughout: every result is exact Φ on
small Boolean models, evidence about the models, separated from claims about real organizations by a
validation gap that no amount of computing closes.

## The arc

The program built an instrument and a structural law, spent a long middle mapping the measure's own
internal geometry, then turned outward to a concrete application that produced a stated domain law, and
finally audited and corrected itself.

### Phase 1 — The instrument and the structural law (the eight findings, Q43, Q45)

The founding move borrowed exact IIT-4.0 Φ as a dyadic/triadic classifier: a coordination form is
irreducible, and demands algorithmacy, exactly when Φ over the minimum-information partition is positive.
The census pinned the landscape: only 9.4% of strict-mediation three-node forms are triadic. The eight
findings reduced to one law: a form is triadic iff every party is bound into a single irreducible joint
determination. Substitutability of any party collapses it, mediation depth preserves it, cheap proxies do
not recover it, and core membership requires bidirectional coupling plus graded pivotality. Q43 ran the
first question end to end through the protocol and refuted a 1967 textbook ordering of organizational
interdependence, establishing that the harness produces real results, including unwelcome ones.

### Phase 2 — The seam and typing micro-thread (Q49–Q62)

A long, rigorous, inward-looking excursion into the fine structure of the minimum-information partition:
where the partition cuts (the seam), how the seam relates to partition-type names, normalization geometry,
directed cut edges, and the excluded singleton. The thread closed cleanly at Q57–Q61: recipient direction
fixes both outer cuts, and the excluded cut carries no signal beyond the tied complement once the seam and
type are known. The phase deepened mastery of the measure itself rather than of any application.

### Phase 3 — The outreach application and synthesis (Q63–Q73)

The pivot outward, sparked by a discussion of agent-mediated outreach. Ten cycles built a theory of when
agent-mediated outreach is irreducible. Q63–Q64 established joint determination and the breadth law
(Φ = n−1 for an all-binding campaign). Q65–Q67 took up depth, core localization, and reciprocity, and
produced the program's three refutations, one of which (Q66) corrected the study before it (Q65). Q68–Q70
gave the social result: a bidirectionally-coupled triage agent displaces the sender; when both sides
delegate the two agents become the irreducible core; but only while the agents are specific and required.
Q71–Q72 showed the verdict is robust to noise and not recoverable by any cheap proxy. Q73 stated the
five-pillar law, after which a full critical review caught and corrected the program's own overclaims:
Q65's superseded directional reading, Q69's frozen-spectator framing, and Q73's hardcoded synthesis pillar.

## What is established

1. A reproducible methodology. Hypotheses are fixed before computing, every number reproduces from a
   committed script (a hundred CI checks), about a third of results are nulls or refinements, and the
   program has now demonstrated that it audits and corrects itself.
2. A structural law that survives breadth, depth, reciprocity, delegation, substitutability, and noise.
3. A clean instrument with known limits, validated on its own controls every run.

## What the program is not yet

- **Anchored.** Every result is evidence about a model. No empirical contact exists anywhere in the 237
  probes. This is the largest single gap.
- **Settled at the verdict boundary.** The deepest results, Q65's localization and Q69's displacement,
  live exactly where the whole-system verdict and the maximal complex diverge, and that boundary invites
  overclaim, as the review showed.
- **Rich in state.** Parties are binary and updates are deterministic; Q71 took the first step into noise.
  Real coordination is graded, stochastic, and temporal.
- **Past the ceiling.** Exact Φ caps near ten to twelve elements, and the cheap-proxy escape route fails
  (Finding 7, Q72).

## A path forward

Ordered by leverage, mapped to the fifty-question agenda (`RESEARCH_AGENDA_50_V2.md`, categories A–J) and
detailed as runnable questions in `RESEARCH_AGENDA_Q74.md`.

1. **Harden the construct (agenda J).** Pin down when the maximal-complex reading is the right verdict and
   how whole-system and complex should be reported together, so claims like "the agents are the core"
   cannot overreach. Cheap, computational, and it cleans the foundation the review exposed. Do this first.
2. **Cross the validation gap (agenda G, I).** The defining next chapter. Until one result touches data,
   the program stays interesting in-silico. The cheapest anchor is a behavioral experiment for the outreach
   forms, testing whether the dyadic/triadic verdict predicts a coordination-difficulty gap, or an analysis
   of real interaction logs where a proxy can be checked against an exact-Φ ground truth on the small units.
3. **Richer state (agenda A, B, C).** Lift the binary, deterministic, synchronous forms to multivalued,
   stochastic, and temporal, and test whether the outreach law generalizes or breaks. Either is informative.
4. **The estimation frontier (agenda E).** Train a learned surrogate on the exact-Φ outreach corpus
   (built on `proxy_bridge/`) to predict the verdict past the size ceiling, the neuroscience playbook of an
   exact teacher and a cheap student, then test it on real-scale agent pipelines.
5. **Multi-agent extensions (agenda H).** Markets of agents, agent coalitions, the platform as an agent
   operator, and adversarial agents that try to stay outside or seize the core. The two-agents-as-core
   result is the seed of a sub-program.
6. **The organization-theory bridge (agenda I).** Connect the outreach law to the literacy/algorithmacy
   construct and to the account of the platform as a third coordination mechanism, giving the instrument a
   theoretical home.

The order is the recommendation: harden the construct, then commit to the empirical anchor, because every
other direction compounds in value once one result has crossed from the model to the world.

whole program is synthesized in the capstone, [`essays/algorithmacy_outreach_paper.md`](essays/algorithmacy_outreach_paper.md).
